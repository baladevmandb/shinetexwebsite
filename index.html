Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantities vary by product type. For standard items, MOQs typically start at 500-1000 pieces. For custom designs, the MOQ may be higher. Please contact our sales team for specific product MOQs.

Do you offer samples before bulk orders?

Yes, we provide samples for all our products. Sample fees may apply but are often credited back on bulk orders. Sample production typically takes 3-7 business days plus shipping time.

What is the production lead time?

Standard production lead time is 15-25 days after sample approval, depending on the product type and quantity. Rush orders may be accommodated with an additional fee. During peak seasons, lead times may be extended.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to customers worldwide. We work with various international shipping carriers and can arrange door-to-door delivery. Shipping costs and delivery timeframes vary by destination.
